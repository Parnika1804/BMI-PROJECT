What Are Soulbound Tokens (SBTs)?

Soulbound Tokens (SBTs) are non-transferable digital badges earned by student mentors on this platform. These tokens serve as a recognition of their effort, reliability, and impact on users they help.

Mentors receive SBTs based on:

  • Number of successful mentorship sessions
  • Positive feedback from users
  • Quick response time

The more SBTs a mentor earns, the more trusted and experienced they appear to users.
